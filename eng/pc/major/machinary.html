Construction Machinery

Construction Machinery Projects

The main focus of this business is construction equipment,
the lifeblood of earthworks, we focus our attention on operating
top-quality and well-maintained construction machinery.

Our quality management system is based on the ISO9001/KSA9002
standards, ensuring outstanding product quality and precision.

We also adhere strictly to the Korean Occupational Safety
and Health Management System (KOSHA-MS) to ensure that all of
our construction is safe.
