Health and Safety

We value health
and safety above all else.

Samho Development’s management recognizes
the inherent worth of human life and therefore makes
responsible decisions regarding our laborers, customers,
citizens, and executives and employees.
Safety, health and the environment are our top priorities
when managing our company and we adhere to the following
Occupational Health and Safety Management Policy
in order to create a safe and pleasant work environment.

All executives and employees pledge their on-going
commitment to health and safety and strive to set
a good example in order to establish a culture that
promotes safety and health.

By adhering strictly to health and safety laws and
regulations and maintaining strong values and goals
for health and safety, we ensure that our labourers,
clients and employees are able to continue their
regular employment.

Ensure workers are consulted about health and
safety policies and measures and raise safety
awareness through health and safety training prior
to commencing work.

Compulsory risk evaluation before any work
commences, followed by corrective action where
necessary.

Create and maintain an optimal work environment
that is comfortable and safe.

Periodically review and improve workplace health
and safety processes.

This policy has been implemented as part of
Samho Development's commitment to responsible
management, and all our executives and employees undertake
to create a safe work environment and actively prevent
accidents by acting in accordance with this policy.
