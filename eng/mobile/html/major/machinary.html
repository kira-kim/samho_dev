Construction Machinery

Construction Machinery Projects

The main focus of this business is construction equipment,
the lifeblood of earthworks, we focus our attention on operating
top-quality and well-maintained construction machinery.

Our quality management system is based on the ISO9001/K-
SA9002 standards, ensuring outstanding product quality and
precision.

We also adhere strictly to the Korean Occupational Safety
and Health Management System (KOSHA-MS) to ensure that all
of our construction is safe.

Our construction machinery business
makes every effort to ensure that
our customers receive only the
highest-quality products
and the best service.
